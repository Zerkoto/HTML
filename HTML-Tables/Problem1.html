Cats are awesome!
The domestic cat is a small, usually furry, domesticated, and carnivorous mammal. They are often called a house cat when kept as an indoor pet, or simply a cat when there is no need to distinguish them from other felids and felines. Cats are often valued by humans for companionship, and their ability to hunt vermin and household pests.
